The Problem

Students in remote and rural areas often lack access to the same quality of STEM educational resources as their urban counterparts. This educational gap is compounded by a lack of engaging, interactive digital tools that can function in low-connectivity environments.

The Journey: From Ideation to Solution

lightbulbIdeation & Wireframing

From our 4 initial ideations, the Career RPG concept proved most effective at communicating the practical application of school subjects and gamifying engagement. Recognizing its high processing demands were a critical flaw for rural contexts, we synthesized its strengths into a more feasible concept to undergo user testing.

The Solution: High-Fidelity Designs

Our final design, CareerCourse, is a gamified LMS for students in rural and low-socioeconomic areas that addresses the educational gap by connecting school curriculum to real-world value. Using a motivational avatar system, students complete coursework to level up, unlocking the core feature: virtual excursions hosted by professionals for direct exposure to STEM careers.
