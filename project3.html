One Shop —
AI-Driven Fashion Shopping App

Role: Lead UI/UX Designer/Researcher
Duration: 13 weeks
Key Design Goal: Increase customer satisfaction with online purchases to reduce product returns.
View the Design Report
quiz

The Problem

Online fashion shoppers often struggle with decision-making due to the inability to visualize how clothes will fit and look on them. This uncertainty leads to high return rates and a frustrating user experience.

attribution

Chloe, The Online Stylist

"A fashion-conscious shopper who buys a lot of her clothes online but is tired of the high return rate due to sizing."

Needs: To see how clothes fit on her body type

Goals: Easy returns are a must

Frustrations: Inaccurate size charts

Frustrations: Clothes looking different in person

groups

My Role & The Team

On this project, I was responsible for the following key areas:

  • Market Analysis
  • User Journey Mapping
  • Persona Development
  • Concept Prototyping

I collaborated with a team of 4 student designers.

document_search

Research & Discovery

Analyzed 5 major fashion e-commerce apps and surveyed 50 online shoppers. A key insight was that 'fit' was the #1 concern, cited by 95% of participants.

"I've bought the same size from the same brand and had it fit completely differently. I just don't trust it anymore."

moving

The Journey: From Ideation to Solution

lightbulbIdeation & Wireframing

Brainstormed features to address the 'fit' problem, leading to the core concept of an AI-driven app, powered by a user's customizable avatar.

task_altUsability Testing

Presented a concept prototype to a panel of peers and a professor. The feedback was generally positive on the core concept, but concerns were raised about the backend feasibility.

Key Finding:

"While the user need was strong, the main point of concern was for the real heavily on the accuracy of the AI visualization."

model_trainingKey Iteration

The project scope was refined to focus heavily on the avatar creation and personalization tool pipeline.

The Change:

We conducted a detailed, polished setup of the product's persona. This led to a better quality of the final product and increased user confidence with the concept's integrity.

arrow_drop_down
design_services

The Solution: High-Fidelity Designs

We developed a user-centered mobile app that uses AI to allow shoppers to visualize clothing on a personalized avatar. By providing a more realistic and interactive shopping experience, we aimed to increase user confidence and reduce returns.
